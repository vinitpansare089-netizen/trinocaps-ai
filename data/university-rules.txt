Attendance Policy
Students must maintain at least 75 percent attendance to appear in examinations.

Hostel Rules
Hostel gates close at 10 PM. Students returning late must inform the warden.

Library Rules
The library is open from 9 AM to 8 PM on working days.

Exam Revaluation
Students can apply for revaluation within 7 days of result declaration.

Leave Policy
Students must submit leave applications through their department.
